About the olympiad

Olympiad "Lisenok"

  • Holds the first time in 2023
  • Olympiad in Mathematics
  • For students of grades 1-4
  • Full-time format
  • Oral delivery of tasks
  • All participants receive certificates
  • Prize-winners and winners receive gifts

Olympiad mathematics is fun, interesting and useful!

Answers to popular questions

In this year, only 2nd grade students of VML csn take part in olympiad.
The Olympiad is held in December (a more precise date will be written on the website).
Venue - G.Vologda, Proletarskaya str., 18.
Participation in the Olympiad "Lisenok" will help to interest the child in Olympiad mathematics, and also, the student will try himself in writing Olympiads and have an interesting time.
Participation in the Olympiad is free of charge.
You can prepare for the Olympiad by solving the options presented on the website In addition, you can start studying in the "Lisenok" class.
Registration opens in September-October and lasts until December. More precise dates will be indicated on the main page of the site.
The Olympiad is held in full-time format. The delivery of tasks is oral. The Olympiad lasts 1.5 hours.
You can contact the organizers by e-mail, telegram or phone number.
