The Olympiad in mathematics "Lisenok" will be held for the first time in 2023-2024! An olympiad holds for 1-4th garde students, but unfortunatly, in this year only 2nd grade students, who study in the VML can participate in olympiad.

"The Lisenok" is not only an Olympiad, but also an opportunity to solve interesting mathematical problems, find new friends and, most importantly, communicate with smart, loving people. In order not to miss registration, keep an eye on news on the site.

Attention! Open registration for the Olympiad mathematics class. The deadline for submitting an application is December 20. An olympiad holds at 24th of December.

"Lisenok" is an Olympiad in mathematics for elementary school. Our goal is not only to organize an event, but also to show children that mathematics is interesting!
